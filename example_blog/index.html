Meet Bethany

Bethany came from China in 1995 as a graduate student attending the University of Florida. She moved to Silicon Valley in 1997 and found her first job as a software engineer. Later on, she changed career to teaching. Currently, she teaches technology and online career training courses at the Mountain View Adult School.

Bethany is blessed with a happy family and balanced life. Her favorite time is spent at Lake Cunningham jogging, strolling, and watching sunset. Besides teaching, she enjoys reading, gardening, traveling, meeting friends, and serving the community.
